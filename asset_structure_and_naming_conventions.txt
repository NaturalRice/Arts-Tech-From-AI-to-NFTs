Asset Directory Structure:
1. assets/
   - images/ (e.g., character_hero.png, bg_forest.png)
   - audio/ (e.g., background_music.mp3, sfx_click.wav)
   - sprites/ (e.g., enemy_slime.png, npc_merchant.png)
   - backgrounds/ (e.g., dungeon.png, village.png)

Naming Conventions:
- Characters: character_[name].png
- Backgrounds: bg_[name].png
- Items: item_[name].png
- Audio: [type]_[name].mp3/.wav (e.g., sfx_click.wav, music_main.mp3)
